Packageorg.as3lib.kitchensync.action.group
Interfacepublic interface IActionGroup extends IAction , ISynchronizerClient , IEventDispatcher
Implementors AbstractActionGroup

Since : 2.0

A group of other actions that is itself an action. An action group allows several child actions to execute as if they were a single unit. The children of a group can be any kind of IAction including other IActionGroup objects. The group will be responsible for executing all of the standard controls for an action on its children and will keep track of the order of the children. For example, pausing and unpausing a group should pause and unpause the children in the group. Note: the duration for groups should be 0.

totalDuration:int  [read-only]

Returns the total duration of all the children within the group. This is calculated by adding upthe sum of each child's duration and delay (but does not include the delay of the group itself). Note, some actions, such as KSAsyncFunctions, may not report accurately on their true duration.
